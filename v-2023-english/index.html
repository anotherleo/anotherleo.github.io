ABOUT ME

I'm Leo Thomas Monteiro, a front-end web developer based in the south of Brazil.

With focus on delivering high-quality results, I have collaborated on numerous web development projects for clients with high standards. I'm counting up about 50 projects by now.

I strive to do my best in every project I engage, seeing challenges as paths to achieve new edges of abilities.

I work remotely and full-time to LDNA S/A, a digital agency, and take my free time to freelancing projects and study new tech skills. All of what I do is made with passion, a lot of coffee and support from my incredible wife.

I'm a self-taught developer, with a knack for diving deep into documentations and explore concepts through hands-on experimentation.

Being self-taught means I have focus and discipline to achieve tangible results. I know how to navigate the overwhelming information overload that we find ourselves all the time, with a good ability to break down the large chunks of information and transform them into bits of structured knowledge.

As my knowledge grew and I've learned a bit of interesting things, I decided to share some thoughts in a CSS blog.
